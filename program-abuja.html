Program Overview

This webinar created a technical dialogue platform for evidence review, interpretation quality, and practical implications for maternal and infectious disease research.

The session connected epidemiological findings to policy and programmatic decision contexts in health systems.

Activities & Value Delivered

Activities included paper synthesis, methodological critique, comparative interpretation of subgroup findings, and expert discussion on evidence limitations.

Value delivered includes improved critical appraisal capability, stronger interpretation confidence, and clearer translation of findings for policy and research audiences.
