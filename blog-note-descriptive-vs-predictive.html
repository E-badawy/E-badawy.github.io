Choosing Between Descriptive and Predictive Workflows

Category: Modelling | Read: 7 min | Status: Published

Decision criteria for selecting fit-for-purpose analysis depth under real operational constraints.

Key Points

  • Start with decision intent: explain current performance or forecast future outcomes
  • Use descriptive workflows when data quality or historical depth is limited
  • Choose predictive workflows where forward-looking risk or allocation decisions are required
  • Assess implementation readiness, not only model feasibility
  • Document uncertainty boundaries and fallback descriptive options
