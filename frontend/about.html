CIGMA Data Profiler

CIGMA Data Profiler is a data quality and readiness platform for fast ingestion checks, profiling, anomaly detection, and model-readiness validation before analytics and ML.

What It Covers

  • Schema and type checks across common enterprise formats.
  • Quality scoring for missingness, duplicates, outliers, and drift.
  • Root cause and blast-radius insights with remediation guidance.
  • Light EDA and baseline ML benchmarking for quick feasibility.
