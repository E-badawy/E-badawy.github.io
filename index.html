About Me

A Multi-disciplinary Data Scientist and Business Intelligence expert, with deep expertise in transforming data into actionable insights. My work spans health analytics (Epidemiology and Bioinformatics), Machine learning, scientific research, Business intelligence, and predictive modeling; integrating both scientific precision and strategic decision thinking.

I build practical, scalable solutions using R, Python, SQL, Power BI, QGIS, and automation tools, with projects ranging from CRM systems and inventory automation to dashboards, web-scraping pipelines, and health-data modelling. My portfolio reflects a blend of scientific rigour, operational insight, and technical versatility — supported by experience in project management, systems design, and real-world implementation workflows.

My approach combines analytical rigour with engineering discipline. I build across the full AI lifecycle:

  • Data extraction, cleaning, and transformation using SQL and Python
  • Exploratory data analysis, statistical modelling, and feature engineering (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn)
  • Machine learning and deep learning model development (Scikit-learn, PyTorch)
  • API development and deployment (FastAPI, Docker)
  • Integration of LLM-powered workflows and automation (LangChain, modern AI frameworks)
  • Monitoring, optimisation, and continuous system improvement
