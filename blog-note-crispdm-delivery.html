CRISP-DM in Public Programme Delivery Pipelines

Category: Methods | Read: 6 min | Status: Published

How I adapt CRISP-DM phases to match institutional timelines and stakeholder decision windows in real programme environments.

Key Points

  • Translate the business-understanding phase into stakeholder and policy context mapping
  • Use data-understanding to profile source quality before committing to model pathways
  • Keep modeling cycles short and tied to a decision checkpoint, not only model metrics
  • Evaluate with operational usability criteria alongside statistical performance
  • Deploy with governance notes, ownership mapping, and post-deployment monitoring
