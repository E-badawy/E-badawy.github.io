Who We Are

Cigma Intelligent Data Solution partners with businesses and programmes to investigate operational data, diagnose bottlenecks, and deploy intelligence systems that improve performance, compliance, and decision flow.

Mission

To deliver decision-ready analytics, automation, and AI integrations that help organisations move from data noise to operational clarity, measurable performance, and strategic confidence.

Vision

To become the most trusted intelligence partner for businesses and programmes seeking resilient, evidence-driven operations across Africa and global markets.

Service Model

  • Business process automation (inventory, CRM, revenue optimisation).
  • Data-driven analytics (dashboards, pipelines, predictive modelling).
  • AI integration (LLM workflows, intelligent assistants, automation).
  • Monitoring & evaluation frameworks (ToC, LogFrame, KPI mapping).
  • Data mentorship and training for teams.

Typical Deliverables

  • Executive dashboards with KPI tracking and automated alerts.
  • Weekly decision reports and performance summaries.
  • Forecasting models and decision intelligence outputs.
  • Data quality audits, profiling, and governance checklists.
  • Training playbooks for internal data teams.

Engagement Flow

  1. Context audit and stakeholder alignment.
  2. Data mapping and workflow design.
  3. Automation + analytics deployment.
  4. Training, handoff, and continuous improvement.
